Football
Football Rules and Regulations:
1. Each team has 11 players, including a goalkeeper.
2. A match consists of two 45-minute halves.
3. The game starts with a kickoff and is played using a round ball.
4. Offside rules apply to prevent unfair advantages.
5. Fouls and misconduct result in free kicks, penalty kicks, or yellow/red cards.

Basketball
Basketball Rules and Regulations:
1. Each team consists of five players.
2. The game is played in four quarters of 12 minutes each.
3. Dribbling and passing are essential for ball movement.
4. Fouls result in free throws or loss of possession.
5. The team with the most points at the end wins.

Cricket
Cricket Rules and Regulations:
1. Each team has 11 players, including batsmen and bowlers.
2. The game consists of innings where teams bat and bowl.
3. Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between wickets.
4. Bowlers aim to get batsmen out using various techniques.
5. The team with the most runs at the end of the match wins.
